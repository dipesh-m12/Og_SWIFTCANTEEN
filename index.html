About Us

Welcome to SwiftCanteen! We are a startup founded by students at Pillai College of Engineering in Panvel. Our goal is to revolutionize the way students access food on campus. As a team of dedicated students, we understand the challenges of managing meal options efficiently and aim to provide a seamless experience for all users.

Our founder, Dipesh Mishra, is currently pursuing a B.Tech in Information Technology in his third year. We are based in Navi Mumbai and are passionate about leveraging technology to improve student life. SwiftCanteen offers a variety of meal options with easy ordering and quick delivery.
